About

  • I am a graduate student at Seoul National University and a member of the Autonomous Robot Intelligence Laboratory (ARIL).
  • My research focuses on Non-Line-of-Sight (NLoS) Perception and Sensor Fusion using radar, acoustic, and visual sensors to enable robust situational awareness in autonomous systems.
  • My broader interests include Robotics, AI perception, Multimodality, and Human-Robot Interaction.
